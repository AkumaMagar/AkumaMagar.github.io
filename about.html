Aspiring Animator from London

From a young age, I have been captivated by the magic of animation by how it can bring no existing things into form life. To pursue my dream to become an animator, I have been learning various animation techniques, from handmade drawing to digital 2D and 3D animation. I have also explored character design, storyboarding, background design, illustration, concept art, rigging, texturing, lighting, and graphic designs. I am currently studying Animation at University for the Creative Arts since September 2022. Studying at UCA has expanded my horizon including the concept of animation.

The work I have created so far is displayed on this website as well as my Instagram, linked above. Feel free to get in touch with me if you have any queries or ideas for collaborating.
